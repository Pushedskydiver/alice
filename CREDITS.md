## Ralph — the technique that powers Clancy

**Geoffrey Huntley** ([@GeoffreyHuntley](https://x.com/GeoffreyHuntley))
https://ghuntley.com/ralph/

Geoffrey coined the Ralph technique — at its core, a loop that gives an
AI coding agent a fresh context window for every task, solving context rot
through simplicity. Clancy (one of the agents Alice recommends) is built on
and extends this foundational idea for team-based, board-driven development.
